website

This website exists only for me, much like the Valley itself.

Discord is an unappealing archive platform, so I have migrated my journal here primarily so that my dreams can be browsed with greater ease. There are also some funny widgets, divination tools and stuff about me. I do not care about optimization, and intend to cram as much ridiculous garbage, cool effects and secret buttons into the pages as I can think of.
